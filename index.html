The Building

The 1917 Jones Motor Company building, restored as a venue.

Before Gather on Broadway, 139 N Broadway was the Jones Motor Company building, built in 1917 during the Broadway District's early-automotive era. Tommy and Jyll Everman spent years restoring it into a dedicated event space, keeping the bones of the original structure intact.

20-foot wood ceilings

Original ceiling height from the 1917 build, left intact to give the room a scale few venues in Northeast Wisconsin can offer.

Exposed steel trusses

The original 1917 structural trusses, left visible after restoration. They define the look of the room and tell the story of the building's first life.

Original brick walls

A century of Broadway District history in the walls. No paint, no coverings, just the original brick that has been there since 1917.

Broadway District address

139 N Broadway puts your guests within easy walking distance of Green Bay's downtown hotels, parking, and restaurants. A convenient corner for out-of-town visitors and locals alike.

Our Story

A husband-and-wife team who put everything into one historic building.

Gather on Broadway is owned and run by Tommy and Jyll Everman. In 2016, they took on the 1917 Jones Motor Company building at 139 N Broadway and turned it into a wedding and events venue in the heart of Green Bay's Broadway District.

Jyll serves as Executive Chef, personally leading the kitchen team and developing the menus served to every couple and corporate client. Since January 2025, she has also been an on-air host of Fox 11 Living, Green Bay's long-running local lifestyle program. Her food background is woven into every event Gather hosts.

Tommy runs the operations side: the building, event production, and the daily work that makes a 107-year-old structure function as a modern venue. The ownership team also includes Maddy, who leads hospitality, and Erin, who handles events, both reachable directly through gatheronbroadway.com.
